TrendMosaic – Multi-Source Tech Trend Aggregator

TrendMosaic is a platform that aggregates and analyzes tech industry trends by leveraging discussions from various online platforms. It utilizes advanced language models to extract sentiment, summarize discussions, and highlight emerging technologies.

Explore more about TrendMosaic on its landing page or try the live demo.

Underdogged – Football Prediction Engine

Underdogged is a football analytics tool that uses historic match data and engineered features to predict match outcomes with a lightweight machine learning model. It includes context such as recent team form and head-to-head results to help users explore and understand likely outcomes.

Check out Underdogged on its landing page or try the live demo .
